About Us

Rankings are a fundamental tool used to help people understand the relative merit of objects or choices. They are employed to simplify decision making when the number of factors impacting choice is large. Our mission is to provide a user-driven ranking system to allow non-expert users to better understand the explanatory power of ranking models for socioeconomic outcomes.

We accomplish this in two ways. By incorporating ranking algorithms in highly usable interfaces to provide a non biased visualization, to understand existing rankings and to create ranking models which reflect user’s personal intuition and evaluation. By utilizing machine learning, we develop a tool to automatically rank objects in the context of information retrieval.
