RANKIT

An easy, step by step, ranking tool to vizualize data!

Get Started

Step 1: Pick a Dataset


Pick a dataset or start importing your dataset by clicking “”Start Ranking” and navigating to import. File must be of JSON type.

Step 2: Building your Ranking

Step 2: Explore your Ranking

Now you can rank your data into three different types of rankings.

  • Categorical Comparison: Rank your entities using three Categories. Each Object in the category is considered the same ranking.
  • List Comparison: Rank all your entities in one list by preference.
  • Pairwise Comparison: Compare your entities in pairs to judge which of each entity is preferred, has a greater amount, or whether or not the two entities are identical.

Drag and drop your data objects to the respective ranking. Once you're done click “Rank!”

Now you can customize your Ranked data with different visualizations based on the options presented.

Visualizes the rank and allows users to change the priority of one attribute over the other by interacting with the interface. The Explore tool reacts instantly to any changes giving an opportunity to visualize and observe how the ranking was affected

When you're finished save results and export.

You're Ready to RankIt!
